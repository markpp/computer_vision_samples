 
 
** This sample demonstrates how to grab images and disparity map with the ZED SDK and link the results with OpenCV.
!! GPU->CPU readback is time consuming. This samples is not designed to operate in RealTime. !!


** Both images and depth/disparity map are displayed with OpenCV                                 
** Most of the functions of the ZED SDK are linked with a key press event (using opencv)
 


This sample needs OpenCV
